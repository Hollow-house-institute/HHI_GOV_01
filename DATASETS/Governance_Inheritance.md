# Dataset Governance Inheritance — HHI_GOV_01

All datasets produced, distributed, or derived under Hollow House Institute
inherit governance authority from HHI_GOV_01.

## Inheritance Rules
- Applies to all formats (CSV, JSON, text, vectors, embeddings)
- Applies to derived, filtered, or transformed datasets
- Governance obligations persist through redistribution

## Non-Removal Clause
Removal or alteration of governance references constitutes non-compliance
and may trigger license violation.

## Canon
HHI_GOV_01 is the sole governance authority for datasets governed herein.
