# Relationship to HHI-GOV-01

HHI-GOV-01 is the canonical governance standard for Hollow House Institute.

Any repository, dataset, framework, or operational system claiming governance,
auditability, compliance, or authority within Hollow House Institute MUST
derive, reference, or explicitly declare deviation from HHI-GOV-01.

## Scope of Authority
HHI-GOV-01 governs:
- Decision authority boundaries
- Escalation integrity
- Accountability persistence over time
- Evidence requirements for review and enforcement

## Non-Substitution Clause
No policy document, ethical guideline, or operational checklist may substitute
for the governance infrastructure defined in HHI-GOV-01.

## Change Control
Changes to this relationship require:
- Versioned update to HHI-GOV-01
- Public changelog entry
- Explicit governance tag
